About Me

Hi! I’m Amir, an enthusiastic architect deeply devoted to design. Echoing the words of Walter Gropius, the influential German architect and founder of the renowned Bauhaus school, “It defines the scope of an architect’s work from a spoon to a city!” I revel in the art of design and relish exploring diverse design realms, which has led me to embark on a more professional journey in graphic design. As a graphic designer, I strive to make life more beautiful and easier in the simplest way possible, believing that big things happen from the combination of simple events. To me, design is a vast world, and graphic design is the simplest way to showcase it to people. I have come to Toronto to pursue my design and artistic goals, constantly experiencing and learning new things every day.
